Project Page

In this program, I worked with my group to complete the design and development of the Leadina website. We first analyzed the users of this website and determined the color scheme and style to be used based on their preferences and needs. We realized that the client of this website is looking for a healthy life and likes organic food, so we used green as the main color to emphasize the "healthy" theme. Then we used different colors of yellow to make the whole page more coherent and aesthetically pleasing.

Basic Branding Ideas

  1. This is a clothing sales company. We provide online shopping, logistics and after-sales service. Detailed information and pictures of products can be found on this company's website. This company offers delivery services and can check the logistics information. At the same time, users can return or exchange products that they are not satisfied with.
  2. This company sells fashionable clothes, so it targets young men and women. At the same time it mainly offers online shopping, so it targets people who don't have time to shop but are fashion conscious. On the other hand, this company's products are cheap, so it is suitable for people who are budget conscious.
  3. This company's clothing is designed by designers to meet current fashion trends and popular aesthetics, and can show the buyer's taste. The products are reasonably priced and of reliable quality for most people. The shopping experience of this company is good, users can get all the information they want and buy clothes on the website without going to the store in person. On the other hand, the after-sales service of this company is very good and users can return or exchange the items they are not satisfied with.
